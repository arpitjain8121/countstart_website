What is Abacus?

Abacus is a mechanical aid used for performing rapid arithmetic calculations. It is not a calulator in a sense we use. It consists of a rectangular frame holding a number of vertically arranged rods, on which beads slides up and down.

The use of abacus improves the ability to calculate both on abacus and mentally ,but also provide a beneficial ripple effect on another discipline.

Improvement of Numerical memory

Improvement of memory in remembering images.

Abacus mathematical calculations is considered to be one of the fastest way of doing calculations. Count star learning program is moulded in such a way that one starts visualizing the complete movement of the beads corresponding to the mathematical steps.
